Hypothesis Testing

  1. State hypotheses
  2. Calculate test statistic
  3. p-value
  4. Decision
  5. Conclusion

Hypothesis Testing for \(p\)

  • A pharmaceutical company wants to show that fewer than 20% of patients who use a particular medication experience side effects

  • In a clinical trial with 400 patients, they find 68 experienced side effects

Hypothesis Testing for \(p\)

  • Hypotheses

\[H_0: p \ge 0.20\] \[H_1: p < 0.20\]

From our sample, out of 400 patients, 68 experienced side effects.

\[\hat{p} = \frac{68}{400} = 0.17\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(p\)

  • Distribution of \(\hat{p}\) ASSUMING \(H_0\) is true \[\hat{p} \sim N\Big(0.20, \sqrt{\frac{0.2(1-0.2)}{400}}\Big)\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(p\)

  • Test statistic

\[Z^* = \frac{\hat{p} - p_0}{\sqrt{\frac{p_0(1-p_0)}{n}}} = \frac{0.17 - 0.20}{\sqrt{\frac{0.2(1-0.2)}{400}}} = -1.5\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(p\)

  • p-value

\[H_0: p \ge 0.20\] \[H_1: p < 0.20\]

\[P(Z < -1.5) = normalcdf(-e99,-1.5,~0,~1) = 0.0668\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(p\)

  • Decision:

DO NOT reject \(H_0\) since

\[p-value>\alpha\] \[0.0688>0.05\]

  • Conclusion

There is not signicant evidence to show that fewer than 20% of patients who use a particular medication experience side effects

Hypothesis Testing for \(\mu\)

In 2008, the online travel agency Travelocity reported that summer travelers booked their airline reservations an average of 73.3 days in advance. A random sample of 40 summer travelers in 2010 was selected. The average number of days travelers booked their airline reservations in advance was 78 with a standard deviation of 6.85 days. Using = 0.1, test the hypothesis that the average number of days reservations are booked in advance has changed since 2008.

Hypothesis Testing for \(\mu\)

  • Hypotheses

\[H_0: \mu = 73.3\] \[H_1: \mu \ne 73.3\]

From our sample of 40 summer travelers, we get a mean of 78 and s.d. of 6.85.

Hypothesis Testing for \(\mu\)

  • Distribution of \(\bar{X}\) ASSUMING \(H_0\) is true

\[\bar{X} \sim N\Big(73.3, \frac{6.85}{\sqrt{40}}\Big)\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(\mu\)

  • Test Statistic

\[t^* = \frac{\bar{X} - \mu_0}{s/\sqrt{n}} = \frac{78 - 73.3}{6.85/\sqrt{40}} = 4.339\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(\mu\)

  • p-value

\[H_0: \mu = 73.3\] \[H_1: \mu \ne 73.3\]

\[2 \cdot P(t>4.339) = 2 \cdot tcdf(4.339,~e99,~40-1) = 0.000098\]

Hypothesis Testing for \(\mu\)

  • Decision:

Reject \(H_0\) since

\[p-value < \alpha\] \[0.000098 < 0.1\]

  • Conclusion

There is signicant evidence to show that the average number of days reservations are booked in advance has changed since 2008.
